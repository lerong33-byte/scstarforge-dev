Based on the video, here is exactly what happens to the app layout when the browser window is resized:

1.  **Initial State:** When the window is wide, the layout consists of a main central area displaying a list of items (under "J-SHIP SHOWDOWN") and a distinct, separate panel on the right side that shows detailed statistics (like HP, Armor, Cargo, etc.) for a selected item.
2.  **The Resize:** The user drags the right edge of the browser window inwards, making the window significantly narrower.
3.  **The Result:**
    *   The main central area compresses horizontally, with its internal elements (like the search bar and the list items) reflowing to fit the reduced width.
    *   The most significant change is that the **right-hand statistics panel completely collapses and disappears**. It is hidden from view to accommodate the smaller screen size, leaving only the main list area visible.
